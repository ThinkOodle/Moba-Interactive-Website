MOBA INTERACTIVE has joined the
 Oodle™ team!

Find Out More About This!

As we all know, change is inevitable and over the summer of 2015, we engaged in talks with Oodle, a Cincinnati-based marketing agency located in Loveland, about the possibility of merging our two companies. Oodle has been been growing rapidly and was looking to add mobile development as a core component to their business, as well as experienced, entrepreneurial professionals to add to their team.

After long and careful consideration, we believed this was the best course of action for both of us personally, as well as the business. As of August 31st, 2015 MOBA Interactive was officially acquired by Oodle. We’d like to thank you for all of your support over these past 5 years as it’s been a great ride… and as we turn the page on this chapter, we welcome you to join us on the flip side.
